Our story

We're just ordinary guys from a small town in Russia — Nizhnevartovsk. Alexey and Vladimir. We dreamed of bringing all the flavors of our Russian moms to a big country like the USA. So we packed our bags, our babushkas' recipes, and a whole lot of love for cooking.

Now, at Rusco's, we share the food that tastes like home. We hope you enjoy our dishes as much as we enjoy making them for you. And if you'd like to say a special thank you, you can leave us a message right here on the site. Warm regards, Rusco's.
